	 	\textbf{Question}:
		Let $\vec{A} = 10\vec{I_3}$, where $\vec{I_3}$ is the identity matrix. Find the nullity of $5\vec{A}\brak{\vec{I_3} + \vec{A} + \vec{A}^2}$.


\textbf{Solution:}
Given,
\begin{align}
\vec{A} = 10\vec{I_3},\vec{A}^2 = 10^2\vec{I_3},\vec{A}^3 = 10^3\vec{I_3}
\end{align}
So,
\begin{align}
5\vec{A}\brak{\vec{I_3} + \vec{A} + \vec{A}^2}
\end{align}
\begin{align}
5\brak{\vec{A} + \vec{A}^2 + \vec{A}^3}
\end{align}
\begin{align}
5\brak{10 +100+1000}\vec{I}
\end{align}

So, rank is 3 and nullity is 0.
